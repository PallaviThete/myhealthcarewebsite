FAQ-Frequently Asked Questions

What is Healthy Living actually?
Healthy living means making daily choices that support physical, mental, and emotional well-being. It includes eating a balanced diet, exercising regularly, getting enough sleep, managing stress, and avoiding harmful habits.

What is importance of Medical Nutrition in healthy Living?
Medical nutrition is important in healthy living as it helps prevent and manage diseases through a balanced diet tailored to individual health needs. It supports recovery, boosts immunity, and improves overall well-being.

What is importance of Menu in daily life in healthy Living?
A menu is important in daily life for healthy living as it helps plan balanced meals, control portions, reduce unhealthy food choices, and ensure proper intake of nutrients. It supports consistent healthy eating habits.

What role Clinical expertise play in healthy living?
Clinical expertise plays a key role in healthy living by providing accurate diagnosis, personalized treatment, and professional guidance. It helps in preventing diseases, managing health conditions, and promoting overall wellness.

What we do for Healthy Living?
For healthy living, we should eat a balanced diet, exercise regularly, get enough sleep, manage stress, avoid harmful habits like smoking, and go for regular health check-ups.
